The Phoenix Project

Since their establishment in 1997, TPP has been at the forefront of creating a connected healthcare system. Their innovative SystmOne securely shares 61 million electronic health records, supporting 300,000 users and 7,800 NHS organizations in over 25 diverse settings. This empowers patients by granting secure access to their care records and facilitates groundbreaking research.
